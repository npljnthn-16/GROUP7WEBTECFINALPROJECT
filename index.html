How It Began

We started with one question:
what makes coffee great?

Kapee was born in 2019 from a simple obsession — five friends who spent years drinking bad coffee in good cities, and decided enough was enough. They traveled to origin farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra. They learned to roast. They failed a lot. Then they found something worth sharing.

The first location opened in a 40-square-meter space with secondhand furniture and a single espresso machine. Word spread — not through ads, but through the work. Through the coffee.

Today Kapee operates multiple locations across Metro Manila, but the ethos remains the same: buy good beans, treat them well, and make drinks that give people pause.

What Drives Us

Three things we never compromise on.

01

Origin Transparency

We know exactly where every bean comes from — and we pay fair prices to the people who grow them. No shortcuts, no excuses.

02

Craft Over Speed

We don't rush. Each drink is made with intention. If that means a 4-minute latte, then 4 minutes it is.

03

Space for Everyone

Kapee is for the solo worker, the first date, the old friends catching up. We design every detail with all of them in mind.
